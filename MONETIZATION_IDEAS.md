# Tournament Control Center - Monetization Strategy

A comprehensive brainstorming document exploring revenue opportunities for the Tournament Control Center platform.

---

## Executive Summary

Tournament Control Center is a full-stack tournament management system with unique differentiators: physical Stream Deck integration, multi-display support (TV overlays, brackets, flyers), real-time updates, and a mobile-first participant experience. This document explores monetization strategies ranging from SaaS models to white-label licensing.

---

## 1. SaaS Subscription Tiers

### Tier Structure

| Tier | Price | Target User | Key Features |
|------|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Free** | $0/mo | Hobbyists, testing | 1 tournament/month, 16 participants max, basic displays |
| **Starter** | $19/mo | Weekly locals | 4 tournaments/month, 64 participants, email support |
| **Pro** | $49/mo | Serious TOs | Unlimited tournaments, 256 participants, priority support, analytics |
| **Venue** | $99/mo | Bars/arcades | Multi-room displays, sponsor overlays, API access, phone support |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Esports orgs | White-label, custom integrations, SLA, dedicated support |

### Feature Breakdown

**Free Tier (Loss Leader)**
- Single tournament per month
- 16 participant cap
- 1 display connection
- Basic bracket view
- Community support only
- Challonge branding visible

**Starter Tier**
- 4 tournaments/month
- 64 participants
- 2 display connections
- Ticker messages
- QR code registration
- Email support (48hr response)
- Remove "Powered by" branding

**Pro Tier**
- Unlimited tournaments
- 256 participants
- 5 display connections
- Full analytics dashboard
- Elo ratings & seeding
- DQ timer automation
- Stream Deck support
- Priority email support (24hr)
- Custom flyer uploads

**Venue Tier**
- Everything in Pro
- Unlimited displays
- Sponsor overlay system
- Multi-room support
- Audio announcements
- API access for custom integrations
- Phone support
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom subdomain (yourbar.tourneyops.com)

**Enterprise**
- Everything in Venue
- White-label branding
- Custom domain
- SSO/LDAP integration
- Dedicated infrastructure
- 99.9% SLA
- On-call support
- Custom feature development

## 4. Venue Partnership Program

### Bar/Arcade Revenue Share

**Model:** Partner with bars and gaming venues

**Structure:**
- Venue pays $49-99/month for platform
- Platform takes 5-10% of tournament entry fees collected through system
- Venue gets featured listing in "Find Tournaments" directory

**Value Proposition for Venues:**
- Professional tournament management
- Increased foot traffic on tournament nights
- Sponsor visibility for their own promotions
- Player loyalty program integration
- Beverage sales tracking correlation (premium feature)

### Franchise Model

License the complete setup to new venues:
- $500 setup fee
- $99/month platform fee
- Training and support included
- Marketing materials provided

- 14-day Pro trial for new signups

### Conversion Incentives
- Annual billing: 2 months free (17% discount)
- Referral program: 1 month free per referral
- Early adopter pricing: locked rate for first 100 customers

## 16. Competitive Positioning

### vs. Start.gg
- Lower price point
- Physical display integration (unique)
- Stream Deck support (unique)
- Simpler interface for smaller events
- Focus on local/venue tournaments

### vs. Challonge (Direct)
- Value-add layer on top of Challonge
- Professional display system
- Physical controller support
- Venue-specific features

### vs. Smash.gg
- More accessible pricing
- Not limited to fighting games
- Better for bar/venue environment
- Hardware integration

## 18. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Challonge API changes | High | Build abstraction layer, support multiple backends |
| Low conversion rates | Medium | A/B test pricing, improve onboarding |
| Venue partnership churn | Medium | Long-term contracts, prove ROI |
| Competition from platforms | High | Focus on physical hardware differentiation |
| Support scaling | Medium | Knowledge base, community forums, tiered support |
